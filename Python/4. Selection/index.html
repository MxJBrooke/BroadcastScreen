If else statements are how decisions are made in python code.
Each if statement has a comparison of some form. their are 6 comparitors
  • ==: Exactly equals to
  • !=: Not equal to
  • <=: Less than or equals to
  • >=: greater than or equals to
  • <: Less than
  • >: Greater than
